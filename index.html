What Terpenes Do Snooze and Good Morning Contain?

Cannabinoids are the main chemical compounds made by the cannabis plant, and there are at least 113 active cannabinoids identified in cannabis. Out of all the known cannabinoids, tetrahydrocannabinol (THC) is the ONLY one that’s psychoactive. Cannabinoids work by binding to receptors in our endocannabinoid system (ECS).

Terpenes are organic hydrocarbons found in the essential oils of plants. Early research shows terpenes, which are also found in a myriad of non-cannabis plants, can impact physical and mental well-being.

According to researchers terpenes have been found to work in synergy with cannabinoids like CBD and THC to create what’s known as the entourage effect, which helps determine how the cannabis interacts with your body—often making the plant more effective and additional benefits to the consumer.
